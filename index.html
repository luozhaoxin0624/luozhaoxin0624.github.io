Zhaoxin Luo

I am a final year PH.D candidate at Purdue University Department of Statistics, West Lafayette. My research interest lies in the area of Deep learning, machine learning, statistic modeling and Nature Language Processing. My advisor is Prof. Micheal Zhu. Currently my research focuses on using statistical methods to apply domain knowledge from semantics to deep learning natural language processing models that can improve the small model efficiency in orders of magnitude than existing methods. Before that, I did my bachelors at Department of Mathmatics, Tsinghua University.
